Tianpei Gu

Machine Learning Engineer @ Krea
San Francisco, California

I’ve been working on Generative Models since 2020 with both academic papers and product deployments in related topic. Currently, I’m a Machine Learning Engineer at Krea to build a tool to let everyone uses AI easily. I obtained my Master degree at UCLA where I had great fortune to work with Prof. Bolei Zhou. Prior to my Master study, I did research at Intelligent Vision Group (IVG), Tsinghua University, under the guidance of Prof. Jiwen Lu and Dr. Guangyi Chen. I also had an amazing time at Lexica working on image and video generation models, and SenseTime Research in the topic of GANs and latent space. In December 2020, I received B.S. degree in Computer Science and Mathematics at the University of Maryland.

My research interest is in Computer Vision, especially Generative Models, Diffusion Models and Image/Video Generation.
